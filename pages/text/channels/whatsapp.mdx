## WhatsApp Channel

Connect your Pearl Text agent to **WhatsApp** through your WhatsApp Business account. Pearl replies using the flow, knowledge, and actions defined in your Pearl, across both [inbound](/pages/text/inbound) conversations and [outbound](/pages/text/outbound) campaigns.

The connection runs through **Meta Embedded Signup**: a Meta window opens on the platform, you log in with the Facebook account that manages your business, and Meta links your WhatsApp Business account to NLPearl. You can start it from two places:

- **Settings** → **Text Channels**, then click **Connect WhatsApp**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-channel-location.png" alt="Connect WhatsApp from the Text Channels settings page" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
</Frame>

- From a Pearl's **Overview** page, through the **Add a Channel** chooser.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-add-channel-chooser.png" alt="WhatsApp option in the Add a Channel chooser on a Pearl's Overview page" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
</Frame>

---

### Choose a connection mode

Before connecting, pick how NLPearl should use your number:

| Mode | What it means |
|---|---|
| **Coexistence** - *Keep using WhatsApp on my phone* | Your number stays active in the WhatsApp Business app and keeps its chat history. NLPearl connects alongside the app, so you can keep messaging from your phone. |
| **Dedicated** - *Dedicate this number to NLPearl* | Full API access and higher messaging volume. The number can no longer be used in the WhatsApp Business app, and its app chat history is not kept. |

<Note>
If you already use the number in the WhatsApp Business app, choose **Coexistence** - you keep your app and chat history while Pearl handles conversations on the same number.
</Note>

---

### Connect in Coexistence mode

Before you start, keep your number active in the WhatsApp Business app on your phone, and make sure the app is up to date.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the connection">
    Select **Keep using WhatsApp on my phone**, then click **Connect WhatsApp**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-mode-coexistence.png" alt="Coexistence mode selected in the Connect WhatsApp panel" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in and continue">
    A Meta window opens. Log in with the Facebook account that manages your business and click **Continue**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-meta-intro.png" alt="Meta Embedded Signup introduction screen" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your business assets">
    Pick or create your **Business portfolio**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-business-assets.png" alt="Business portfolio and WhatsApp Business account selection" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>

    Under **WhatsApp Business account**, select **Connect a WhatsApp Business App** - this is what links the number already used in your app.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-connect-app.png" alt="Connect a WhatsApp Business App option selected" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your business information">
    Fill in your business details - they only affect newly created assets.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-business-info.png" alt="Business information form in the Meta window" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your WhatsApp Business phone number">
    Enter the number that is active in your WhatsApp Business app.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-phone-number.png" alt="WhatsApp Business phone number form" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>

    Meta then explains what connecting your existing app means: you keep full access to the app, and account information like your phone number, contacts, and chat history may be shared.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-phone-confirm.png" alt="Details about connecting your existing WhatsApp Business App" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get the QR code">
    Meta shows a QR code and offers to import your business profile, contacts, and the last 6 months of chat history. Keep this window open - you'll scan the code with your phone.

    <Note>
    Group chat history is not shared - only individual conversations are imported.
    </Note>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-qr-code.png" alt="QR code to scan with the WhatsApp Business app" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="On your phone, start the link">
    In the WhatsApp Business app, open the message from the official **Facebook Business** account and tap **Connect**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-app-connect.png" alt="Facebook Business message with the Connect button in the WhatsApp Business app" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>

    Review what connecting to the Business Platform means, then continue.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-app-platform.png" alt="Connect to the Business Platform screen in the WhatsApp Business app" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Scan the QR code">
    Point your phone at the QR code displayed in the Meta window.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-app-scan.png" alt="QR code scanner in the WhatsApp Business app" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>

    Once scanned, the app confirms your number is connected to NLPearl.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-app-success.png" alt="Connection success screen in the WhatsApp Business app" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>

    <Warning>
    Open the WhatsApp Business app at least once every **14 days** to keep the coexistence connection active.
    </Warning>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm your WhatsApp Business account">
    Back in the Meta window, confirm or edit your WhatsApp Business account name and timezone, then finish the signup.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-confirm-account.png" alt="Confirm the WhatsApp Business account name and timezone" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Your number is connected">
    The number is imported automatically and appears with the **App + API** tag: it keeps working in your WhatsApp Business app while Pearl uses it through the API.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-coexistence-connected.png" alt="Connected WhatsApp Business account with a coexistence number tagged App + API" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Connect in Dedicated mode

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the connection">
    Select **Dedicate this number to NLPearl**, then click **Connect WhatsApp**. A Meta window opens - log in with the Facebook account that manages your business and continue through the intro.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-mode-dedicated.png" alt="Dedicated mode selected in the Connect WhatsApp panel" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your business assets">
    Pick or create your **Business portfolio**, and under **WhatsApp Business account** select **Create a WhatsApp Business account** (or pick an existing one).

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-dedicated-create-waba.png" alt="Create a WhatsApp Business account option selected" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your business information">
    Fill in your business name, category, country, and website.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-dedicated-business-info.png" alt="Business information form for the new WhatsApp Business account" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your WhatsApp phone number">
    Choose how you want to be identified when sending messages.

    **Use a display name only** - people only see a display name in the chat, without a phone number.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-dedicated-display-name.png" alt="Use a display name only option with the additional verification notice" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>

    <Note>
    With a display name only, Meta reviews your business name and display name before you can send freely. This can take up to 1 business day; during the review you can send 5 business-initiated test messages every 24 hours.
    </Note>

    **Add a new number** - enter the number to dedicate to NLPearl and verify it by text message or phone call.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-dedicated-new-number.png" alt="Add a new number option with verification by text message or phone call" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>

    <Warning>
    The number must not be registered to any WhatsApp account. If it is, Meta shows an error - migrate the number or disconnect it from the existing account first, then try again (it can take up to 3 minutes for the number to refresh).
    </Warning>

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-dedicated-number-error.png" alt="Error shown when the phone number is already registered to a WhatsApp account" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review permissions and confirm">
    Review what NLPearl will be able to access and do, then click **Confirm**.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-dedicated-permissions.png" alt="Review of the permissions granted to NLPearl" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish the signup">
    Your account is connected. Meta reviews your business against WhatsApp's Commerce Policy and reaches out within 24 hours if there's an issue. You can also add a payment method to your Meta account now or later.

    <Frame>
      <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-dedicated-connected.png" alt="Meta confirmation that the account is connected to NLPearl" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
    </Frame>
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Import your numbers

Once connected, the platform lists the phone numbers of your WhatsApp Business account. Click **Add to account** next to each number you want to use - each one becomes a text channel on the platform.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-dedicated-numbers-list.png" alt="WhatsApp Business account numbers listed with the Add to account button" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
</Frame>

Importing a dedicated number registers it on the WhatsApp Cloud API. If two-step verification is enabled on the number, turn on the **Two-step verification PIN** option in the import dialog and enter the number's **6-digit PIN**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dark_mode/whatsapp-import-number-pin.png" alt="Import WhatsApp number dialog with the two-step verification PIN option" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
</Frame>

<Note>
Coexistence numbers are imported automatically after the QR code scan and never ask for a PIN.
</Note>

To use the channel, open the Pearl you want for [inbound](/pages/text/inbound) or [outbound](/pages/text/outbound) conversations, go to **Settings**, and set the imported WhatsApp number as its **Text Channel**.

<Frame>
  <img src="/images/dark_mode/text-channel-assign-pearl.png" alt="Assign the WhatsApp channel to a Pearl from its settings" className="rounded-[14px] w-full" />
</Frame>

---

### Manage the connection

Everything is managed from the WhatsApp Business account screen in **Settings** → **Text Channels**:

- **Add a number from your WhatsApp app** - connect an additional number in coexistence mode at any time.
- **Remove a number** - disconnects the number from NLPearl and deletes its text channel. Any Pearl using it is paused, and you can import the number again later.
- **Disconnect** - disconnects your WhatsApp Business account from NLPearl. You can reconnect it at any time.
